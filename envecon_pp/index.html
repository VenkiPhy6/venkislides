Methodologies

Two separate methods

  1. Matching
  2. Synthetic Control

Matching

Expectations to satisfy conditional ignorability

  1. Match on as many relevant covariates as possible
  2. Good post-treatment balance from the match without dropping too many treated units
  3. Specific flavor of the matching exercise carried out is robust to changes in hyperparameters:
    • Number of pre-treatment periods matched
    • Matching size
    • Distance metrics

Conclusion

Approval of a management plan leads to a reduction of 0.57–1.26 km${^2}$ year$^{−1}$ (or 0.02%–0.08% year$^{−1}$) of deforestation of a protected area, on average.
