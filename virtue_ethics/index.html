1. Comparing “Cultivate goodness in citizens” against other purposes

  • The oligarch purpose: Purpose of the Wealthy is to rule.
  • The democrat purpose: Purpose of giving all those who have the appropriate birth the right to rule

Neither are the highest although each is valid! We are left with “Cultivate goodness in citizens”.

2. “Cultivate goodness in citizens” as a valid purpose

2.1 Why declare justice only in the polis?

  • Justice is learned by doing.
  • Learning happens by example. Polis exemplifies the doing of justice properly.
    • So not the home or the family but only political organization!

My claim: Virtue ethics is a self-defeating theory

“A self-defeating theory claims that a theory fails even in its own terms, and thus condemns itself.”

Derek Parfit. Reasons and Persons (p. 3). OUP Oxford.

Subclaims inside my claim

Virtue ethics is a self-defeating theory because…

  1. The purpose of a good philosophy is to communicate uncertainty
  2. Virtue ethics fails to reckon uncertainty

1. The purpose of a good philosophy is to communicate uncertainty

  1. Comparison against allegory of the cave: - There is no opening. Darkness is all we have.
  2. In its own terms: - If there is no opening in the cave and we have to live in the darkness how do we do it? It is by communicating about the darkness - the uncertainty. - Justice can’t be declared. Only its uncertainty can be declared.

2. Virtue ethics fails to reckon uncertainty

  • The comparison group is flawed. - It is impossible to enumerate all the possible purposes and hence impossible to compare against all of them. - Consider Peter Singer’s “The Expanding Circle” argument

My claim substantiated with teleological reasoning

Virtue ethics is a self-defeating theory because by …

  1. The purpose of a good philosophy is to communicate uncertainty ✅
  2. Virtue ethics fails to reckon uncertainty ✅

Takeaways

  1. Virtue ethics involves two modes of teleological reasoning - one in which we compare against purposes and another in which we argue for the assumptions for the chosen highest purpose.
  2. My claim - Virtue ethics is self defeating because it fails to live up to the telos of philosophy which is to communicate uncertainty.
